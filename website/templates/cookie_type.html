Support

Support cookies don't typically deal high damage but are critical for keeping your team effective; they apply buffs, remove debuffs, heal or shield teammates, or otherwise enhance overall team performance.

  • Moderate HP
  • Moderate ATK
  • Low DEF
Example Cookies:
  • Jagae Cookie
  • Lime Cookie

Ambush

Ambush cookies specialize in swift, high-impact strikes. Often surprising enemies from the mid or back row with critical damage. They excel at taking out key targets quickly, especially in PvP.

  • Intangible while using skills
Example Cookies:
  • Chili Pepper Cookie
  • Manju Cookie

Charge

Charge cookies are aggressive frontliners that break into enemy lines and often disrupt enemy positioning while dealing decent damage. They're ideal for initiating combat and creating breathing room for your damage dealers or healers.

  • High HP
  • Moderate ATK
  • Moderate DEF
Example Cookies:
  • GingerBrave
  • Cream Soda Cookie

Defense

Defense cookies serve as the team's tank, absorbing damage and protecting fragile allies with high HP and defense. They are essential in maintaining frontline presence and team survivability. With both DMG absorption and buff supplying for allies.

  • High HP
  • Low ATK
  • High DEF
Example Cookies:
  • Strawberry Cookie
  • Fettuccine Cookie

Magic

Magic cookies wield powerful arcane abilities, often dealing significant AoE or single-target burst damage from mid to back rows. Their versatility makes them attackers who can manipulate battles but tend to be fragile.

  • Low HP
  • High ATK
  • Low DEF
Example Cookies:
  • Wizard Cookie
  • Shadow Milk Cookie

Healing

Healer cookies restore teammates' health and often clear status ailments or apply shields to sustain the team in prolonged fights. They ensure your team stays alive through tough PvE or PvP battles.

  • Moderate HP
  • Low ATK
  • Low DEF
Example Cookies:
  • Custard Cookie III
  • Herb Cookie

Bomber

Bomber cookies deliver explosive attacks or widespread damage and sometimes inflict debuffs like stun or poison. They're effective at handling clustered enemies and controlling crowds.

  • Area Damage
  • Low HP
  • High ATK
  • Low DEF
Example Cookies:
  • Cherry Cookie
  • White Lily Cookie

Ranged

Ranged cookies fire from afar, landing multiple attacks over time while staying safe in the backline. Their steady DPS output is reliable and often provides sustained pressure on enemies.

  • Low HP
  • Moderate ATK
  • Moderate DEF
Example Cookies:
  • Pavlova Cookie
  • Pastry Cookie
