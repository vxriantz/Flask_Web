Cookie Run: Kindgom is a role-playing video game that was developed and published by Devsisters in 2021. This is the sixth installment in the Cookie Run franchise. It was revealed on November 28, 2020, and launched globally on January 19, 2021, for Android and iOS. On July 12, 2023, it became available on Google Play Games for PC.

Yes, Cookie Run: Kingdom (CRK) requires a stable internet connection to play. It's an online game where all progress and data are saved on the server.

Yes, Cookie Run: Kingdom (CRK) is a game that can be played for free. It's a construction and combat RPG that allows players to download and enjoy the game for free, without any real money expenditure. Though it includes in-app purchases, spending money isn't required to enjoy the game or advance, creating a player-friendly free-to-play environment.

Cookie Run: Kingdom is available to play on iOS, Windows, and Android devices.

In Cookie Run Kingdom, new cookies are primarily unlocked through the gacha system, events, and by fulfilling specific unlock requirements. You can also hire cookies from other players in certain game modes.

Due to a recent update, the new maximum Cookie level is 90.

Toppings are equippable items that provide stat bonuses to Cookies, significantly impacting their effectiveness in battle. These bonuses can include increased attack, cooldown reduction, defense, and more. Toppings come in different rarities (Common, Rare, Epic) and can be upgraded using Topping Pieces and Coins. Equipping multiple toppings of the same type activates powerful set bonuses, further enhancing a Cookie's build.
