GingerBrave

The protagonist of Cookie Run: Kingdom and a Common Cookie, Gingerbrave (Korean: 용감한 쿠키, “Brave Cookie”), has been available from the game's debut. His role is prioritised to the Front and he is a Charge type.

He is first obtained for free in the game's opening, just like the other characters in Gingerbrave & Friends. As the main character of World Exploration, he guides his companions through the narrative. He also serves as the game's and franchise's poster character, showing up on loading screens, the app's icon, and various other locations. He is the cookie who initiates the first Cookie Legion.

Game Description

GingerBrave was the first Cookie to escape from the Witch's oven. It is unknown how this Cookie came to life, but rumor has it that the Witch accidentally used magic powder instead of ginger powder.

Appearance

GingerBrave is a somewhat petite, slightly broad Cookie made of warm golden brown dough, featuring cerulean eyes and a red grin. On his head is a thin patch of white icing "hair" shaped like a 3. His torso features skull-shaped buttons, and he holds a red-striped white candy cane. He does not wear any form of attire.

Personality

As the main character of his tale, GingerBrave is portrayed as warm and deeply compassionate, embodying a strong sense of fairness. Thanks to his personality, GingerBrave connects easily with almost everyone he meets, even managing to persuade Dark Choco Cookie to join his team against Dark Enchantress Cookie. GingerBrave genuinely cares for all those he considers friendly—or, at the very least, those who are not his direct foes. He is ready to plunge back into peril to rescue his friends, as he firmly adheres to his motto of "no Cookie left behind".

GingerBrave, exhibiting remarkable courage, is regarded as a symbol of hope by his team and other companions because of his steadfast resolve in confronting every obstacle he encounters. In moments when everything appears hopeless, GingerBrave is the one who motivates his companions and unites them to overcome their enemy.

Though emblematic of courage and optimism, GingerBrave's ordeal as one of the earliest to flee the Witch's oven continues to haunt him quietly to this day. He tries to protect others from the dreadful reality of being consumed when suitable to maintain their hopes and spirits uplifted.
