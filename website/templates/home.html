Cookie Run: Kingdom

Cookie Run: Kingdom is Devsisters' eighth game in the Cookie Run series, releasing officially on January 21st, 2021.

It differs from the original's runner gameplay by incorporating real-time battle tactics and city-building, as well as a diverse cast of distinct Cookies and a customisable Kingdom.
