Lime Cookie

Lime Cookie (Korean: 라임맛 쿠키, raim-mat kuki) is an Epic Cookie introduced in the Farers of the Faraway Seas update (v6.7) alongside Jagae Cookie and Manju Cookie. She belongs to the Support category and her role is focused towards the Rear.

Game Description

When baking this Cookie, large amounts of bitter lime peel were used. Perhaps that's why Lime Cookie is so feisty. Her trademark bangs were made with sweet, sweet jelly, and stay perfectly styled no matter how fast or wildly she runs. Don't let her appearance fool you, she is known to be not the friendliest type! There is only one Cookie that she has a weak spot for. She follows that Cookie around and looks up to them. Sometimes Lime Cookie can get pretty jealous and cause a rather uncomfortable situation. It is said that she learned her SMASH! skills from that Cookie…

Skill

Beach Ball Surprise

Blows up a beach ball to grant HP Shield to ally Cookies and Heal them. When entering a battle with the Citrus Squad, Lime Cookie will prepare a surprise gift and add buffs depending on the Citrus Squad Cookies in battle. Additionally, entering a battle with all four of the Citrus Squad Cookies will amplify all buffs. The Citrus Party effect is simplified with more Citrus Squad Cookies in the team. The Citrus Squad will make unforgettable summer memories when they're all together!

Appearance

Lime Cookie is a somewhat shorter Cookie, featuring tan dough, lime-green eyes with two eyelashes on each eye, and lime-green hair. Her hair appears like lime juice and is arranged with short side bangs and big pigtails hanging down to her knees secured by bands that look like lime slices. She dons a lime-green sports bra, a lime-green bikini bottom, and a translucent sarong atop her bikini. She holds a beach ball that is somewhat see-through, featuring a lime slice pattern.
