Strawberry Cookie

Strawberry Cookie (Korean: 딸기맛 쿠키, ttalgi-mat kuki) is a key deuteragonist of Cookie Run: Kingdom and a Common Cookie accessible since its release. She belongs to the Defense type and her role is focused at the Front.

Similar to the rest of GingerBrave & Friends, she is first obtained for free during the game's opening.

She is a central figure in the World Exploration narrative, featured as a regular Story party member and as a Stage Boss with Wizard Cookie in Grandberry Market Stage 11-23.

Game Description

Shyness can be quite advantageous. Why? Strawberry Cookie contains no real strawberries, only the flavoring. Were she as active and energetic as other Cookies, her artificial strawberry fragrance would have worn off in no time! Strawberry Cookie feels so ashamed of her main ingredient, she'd never want the truth to come out. If only she understood that this yummy strawberry scent is one of the many reasons other Cookies love her!

Skill

Don't Come Near!

10 Sec Base Cooldown

Scared Strawberry Cookie spins with her lollipop, dealing damage to all enemies within range.

Appearance

Strawberry Cookie is a relatively small, somewhat thick Cookie featuring brown eyes, each adorned with a single eyelash extending from the top-right corner. She sports a large pink strawberry hoodie featuring drawstrings with puffballs that look like cherries. The hoodie covers her head and mouth. Her pink lollipop protrudes from her jade-green backpack when it's not in use.

Personality

Strawberry Cookie is shy and introverted, frequently tugging at her hoodie's drawstrings when she feels insecure. Strawberry Cookie enjoys playing games in her space and preparing meals for herself.
