Wizard Cookie

Wizard Cookie (Korean: 마법사맛 쿠키, mabeobsa-mat kuki) is a prominent deuteragonist in Cookie Run: Kingdom and a Common Cookie that has been accessible since the game's launch. He belongs to the Magic category and his role is focused in the Middle.

Similar to GingerBrave and Strawberry Cookie, he is obtained for free at the start of the game.

He belongs to GingerBrave's team and serves as a consistent party member in the World Exploration plotline. He temporarily acts as a Stage Boss with Strawberry Cookie in Grandberry Market Stage 11-23.

Game Description

Wizard Cookie came to possess the Witch's Candy Wand by accident (or not?). Within it, he discovered unique magical powers. His popping candy ice cream cone hat is the finishing touch to his wizard look. Frequently used spells include the festive Hocus-Party-Pocus and Abra-Cookie-Cadabra, which brings unexpected luck and fun.

Magic Storm

13 Sec Base Cooldown

Summons a magic lightning storm that moves forward, dealing periodic damage and inflicting Zap to all enemies within range.

Appearance

Wizard Cookie is a Cookie of small stature and an indistinguishable physique. His dough is a pale brown shade. His eyes are cerulean blue, though his white hair obscures the right one. He sports a wizard hat featuring a light brown waffle cone brim. At the tip of the hat is a deep navy shade adorned with yellow stars. He dons a white scarf that nearly resembles a classic wizard's beard.

He dons a deep navy robe, featuring bell sleeves adorned with a white line close to the edge. His Candy Wand matches his height, featuring a wooden handle; the orb resembles a candy and is sky blue with golden stripes

Personality

Wizard Cookie, in different Cookie Run versions, is frequently depicted as possessing a somewhat clumsy and self-conscious demeanor, similar to that of an awkward adolescent. He has been depicted as quick-tempered and eager to demonstrate his magical abilities, even if it involves showcasing his spellcasting talents. In certain stories, he is portrayed as somewhat of a know-it-all, especially regarding magic, and he tends to be direct or even sarcastic, particularly when discussing his own skills.
